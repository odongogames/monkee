Description

You are Monkee. Jump from tree and tree and collect bananas. Don't touch the spikes.


Instructions

Jumping
Click and drag mouse to start aiming. Release mouse to jump.

Walking
Press left arrow or A key to walk left. 
Press right arrow or D key to walk right.

Navigation
Press the ESCAPE button to go back to the main menu.
Click the SOUND button at the bottom of the main menu to toggle the sound.


Game modes

There are two game modes you can select from the main menu

Normal (click play button)
In normal play mode you have 20 seconds to jump through the trees and collect bananas. 
There are spikes on the ground so watch out!

Practice (click practice button)
In practice mode you can jump around and practice your aiming and jumping. No spikes here.


Tips:

- Practice. Practice. Practice
- You don't have to get every banana. Save time by skipping hard to reach
- Don't forget to walk!
